# Internal Company FAQ - 2026

## Support SLAs
- Starter plan: Best effort, typically 72 hours.
- Pro plan: 48 hour response during business hours.
- Enterprise: 8 hour response, 24/7 for critical (P1) issues. Dedicated Slack channel available.

## PTO Policy
Full-time employees accrue 25 days of PTO per calendar year. Up to 5 unused days carry over to the next year. Additional unpaid leave requires manager approval.

## Pricing (as of January 2026)
- Starter: $29 per user per month, billed annually.
- Pro: $99 per user per month, billed annually (16% discount vs monthly).
- Enterprise: Custom pricing, minimum 10 seats. Includes SSO, audit logs, dedicated support, and custom SLAs.

## Expense Policy
Expenses under $100 can be submitted directly. Expenses between $100 and $500 require team lead approval. Anything over $500 must be pre-approved in the finance portal before the spend occurs. All expenses must be submitted within 30 days.

## Remote Work
We are a remote-first company. Core collaboration hours are 10am-2pm in your local timezone on weekdays. There is no fixed office requirement.

## Security & Compliance
We are SOC 2 Type II certified. Enterprise customers can request our latest pen test report and data processing addendum.
